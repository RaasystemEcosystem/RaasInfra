Get Started

Begin your journey with Raasystem and unlock decentralized finance powered by real assets.

1. Create a Wallet

Download Raaswallet or connect your MetaMask wallet to access the Raasystem ecosystem securely.

2. Fund Your Wallet

Acquire Raaskoin through RABEX or transfer supported assets to your wallet for trading and payments.

3. Explore RABEX

Use our AI-driven trading engine to trade Raaskoin, Raastoken, and other assets with precision and automation.

4. Use Raaspay

Make real-time payments using Raaskoin, with instant settlement and optional fiat conversion for merchants.

5. Stake & Govern

Stake Raastoken to participate in community voting and earn ecosystem rewards for your engagement.

6. Access Resources

Visit the Resources page for documentation, API guides, tutorials, and updates to build with or integrate Raasystem.
