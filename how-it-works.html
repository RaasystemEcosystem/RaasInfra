How Raasystem Works

We connect the real world to the blockchain through seamless automation, secure infrastructure, and AI-driven logic.

Step-by-Step Breakdown

1. Asset Creation

Physical assets like gold, coffee, or real estate are tokenized on the blockchain using smart contracts that validate ownership and value.

2. Oracle-Powered Pricing

Live prices from trusted data feeds are fetched off-chain and updated on-chain using a custom XDC oracle, ensuring real-time valuation.

3. Smart Contract Issuance

Raasystem generates secure, auditable smart contracts that handle the terms of trade, asset transfers, and automated settlements.

4. AI Trading Execution

Orders are placed and optimized through RABEX, which uses machine learning to adjust pricing, spread, and liquidity dynamically.

5. Wallet & Payments

Users store, send, and receive digital assets via Raaswallet, and make real-world payments using Raaspay's crypto-to-fiat tools.

6. Real-World Settlement

Whether you're buying goods, paying farmers, or sending remittances, Raasystem ensures instant, final delivery in fiat or goods.

7. Data & Compliance

All transactions are logged immutably and can be filtered for reporting, audits, or integration with government and enterprise systems.

8. Repeat & Scale

With modular APIs and interoperable protocols, Raasystem can be scaled across regions, industries, and use cases in minutes.
