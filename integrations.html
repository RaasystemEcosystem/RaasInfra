Our Integrations

Seamlessly connect with the tools you already use and unlock new possibilities.

Pre-Built Integrations

Ethereum Blockchain

Raasystem integrates directly with the Ethereum network, ensuring smooth interoperability with your existing Ethereum-based assets and contracts.

XDC Network

Leverage the power of the XDC network for fast, low-cost transactions, all while using Raasystem's robust features to manage your assets securely.

Chainlink Oracles

Integrate real-time off-chain data into your blockchain applications, such as live gold pricing and market feeds, with the power of Chainlink’s decentralized oracles.

Raaspay

Enable seamless crypto-to-fiat payment solutions through Raaspay's gateway, providing instant settlement and secure transactions for merchants and customers alike.

Shopify Integration

Accept crypto payments on your Shopify store, powered by Raasystem’s secure payment infrastructure and real-time settlement system.

PayPal Integration

Integrate PayPal into your Raasystem setup for both crypto and traditional fiat payments, allowing you to cater to a broader audience.

Amazon Web Services (AWS)

Power your infrastructure with AWS, seamlessly hosting Raasystem on their reliable cloud platform, and ensuring scalability and security for your applications.

QuickBooks Integration

Integrate Raasystem with QuickBooks for seamless financial management, offering automated expense tracking and report generation directly from your crypto transactions.
